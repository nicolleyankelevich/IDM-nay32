The Vanity

Capturing surrealism in a physical object through the abstraction of a viewer’s self-perception.

Our modern culture has conditioned our sense of self to be warped and distorted by media and social standards- this object means to be a physical embodiment of this effect.

The viewer’s expectations of their appearance must be subverted by the concave and undulating mirrored surfaces, as well as the light reflecting off these materials.
