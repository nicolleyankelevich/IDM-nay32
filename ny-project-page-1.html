Steel Sculpture Work

A kinetic sculpture that requires two users to complete the activating motion.

The structure is worn by to people over their shoulders and around their chest, connected by steel bars on movable joints. The wearers place their wrists into the holders- as they walk closer to each other for an embrace, they are locked from moving further than grazing one anotherâ€™s hands.

This object is a physical representation of the social boundaries placed upon us both because of the pandemic, and the emotional barriers we encounter on a daily basis.
